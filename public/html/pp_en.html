12. What rights regarding your Personal Data do you have?

12.1. Right to access your Personal Data. You have the right to know if and which of your Personal Data we Process.

12.2. Right to correct, update or request deletion of your Personal Data. If your Personal Data is inaccurate, incomplete, or unnecessary, you can request correction or deletion. However, there are situations where we must keep your data, as outlined in clause 5.4.

12.3. Right to restrict processing of your Personal Data. You may request us to restrict processing if you dispute your Personal Data accuracy, find it unlawfully processed, or need it for legal claims.

12.4. Right to object to the processing. You can object to the processing based on legitimate interests, public tasks, direct marketing, research, or statistical purposes.

12.5. Right to Data Portability. We can transfer your Personal Data in a common format for personal use, especially when switching services. This applies when Processing is based on consent or contract performance and done solely by automated means.

12.6. Right not to be subject to automated decision-making (without human intervention), including profiling. You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, that produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you. Exceptions apply as per contract, law, or explicit consent.

12.7. Right to complaint. If you reside in Ukraine, you have the right to file a complaint to the Department for Personal Data Protection of the Secretariat of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine Commissioner for Human Rights: hotline@ombudsman.gov.ua, phone 0800-50-17-20.

12.8. To exercise your rights, submit your request via email at 103advocat@gmail.com with your ID or digital signature, specifying the right, date, and your contact information. We shall respond within 30 days, free of charge.

12.9. You can exercise your rights directly or via an authorised representative, provided that relevant documents are provided by you for such representation.

12.10. Please note that exercising one right does not affect your ability to exercise other rights.
